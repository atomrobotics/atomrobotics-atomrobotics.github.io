X TITLE: Deep Neural in CFD
DESCRIPTION: A Neural Network model for prediction velocity distribution around the circular cylinder using pressure contours. ANSYS Fluent simulations of flow around a cylinder were carried out to establish a dataset to establish the mapping relationship between pressure and velocity.The network layers can process the pressure information by features extraction to obtain the velocity distribution. This architecture can capture features of pressure fluctuations on the cylinder and use them to acquire the velocity field.By comparing the output data of the typical network with Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) results as reference values, it proven that the this deep neural network is able to predict similar trends and achieves a high overall precision. The active prospects of neural network in fluid mechanics have also been demonstrated, which can inspire more kinds of loads prediction in the future.
