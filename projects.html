NN IN CFD

COMPUTER VISION

A Neural Network model for prediction velocity distribution around the circular cylinder using pressure contours. ANSYS Fluent simulations of flow around a cylinder were carried out to establish a dataset to establish the mapping relationship between pressure and velocity.The network layers can process the pressure information by features extraction to obtain the velocity distribution.
