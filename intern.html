INTERNSHIPS

CANADA'21

Six of our team members got selected for the Canadian University internships for 2021 via a program called Globalink Mitacs. A stipend of 3 Lakhs and a Graduate Fellowship of 10 Lakhs approx. is given to each individual selected for this prestigious program. Kudos to all!

USA'21

Two of our team members has got vision based summer research internship for 2021 under the same professor at Arizona State University, USA. The research is based on scene reconstruction using photogrammetry methods for social infrastructures.

TAIWAN'20-21

One of our team member got an research internship in one of the most prestigious taiwan university and worked for 5 months. The research he undertook there aside his supervisor was oriented towards deep learning techniques via segmentation for cancer cell detection.

TAIWAN'20

Three of our team members got summer internship for 2020 in Yuan-Ze University. All three worked on various diverse fields in Robotics- Robotic Arm simulation using ROS; Perception based smart parking system using Segmentation adn CRFs and Machine learning for CFDs using Ansys. Kudos to all!!!
